# greentan Discord bot

Green team rocks!
--------------------------
----------------------------------------------------------------------
`greentan` is a dedicated Discord bot that self assigns roles in the `#self-assign` chat in the Ace of Spades Discord, `greentan` is always evolving and hopefully she will turn more into a general use bot than just a, "self assign bot."

----------------------------------------------------------------------

Usability:
--------------------------
----------------------------------------------------------------------
`.green`  ==  Adds user to the role => Green

`.meme` == Chooses a random Green team meme

----------------------------------------------------------------------

Background:
--------------------------
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Members with this role have access to the `#self-assign` chat and will have the choice between two roles: Green or Blue

However, if the member wants to change their team bias, they will need to contact the owner via **PM or @ mentioning**
