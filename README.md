greentan Discord bot
==========================

Description:
--------------------------

Green team rocks!
--------------------------
`greentan` is a dedicated Discord bot that self assigns roles in the #self-assign chat in the Ace of Spades Discord, `greentan` is always evolving and hopefully she will turn more into a general use bot than just a, "self assign bot."

Usability:
--------------------------
`.green`  ==  Adds user to the role => Green

Background:
--------------------------

`greentan` does more than just self assigning roles, she actually adds the, "Newfag" role to new members

Members with this role will have access to the #self-assign chat and will have the choice between two roles: Green or Blue
